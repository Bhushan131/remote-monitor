# 🚀 BUILD & INSTALL - SIMPLE STEPS

## STEP 1: Prepare Phone

1. On your Android phone:
   - Go to **Settings**
   - Tap **About Phone**
   - Tap **Build Number** 7 times (until it says "You are now a developer")
   - Go back to **Settings**
   - Tap **Developer Options**
   - Turn ON **USB Debugging**

2. Connect phone to PC with USB cable

3. On phone, tap **Allow** when it asks about USB debugging

## STEP 2: Build & Install (ONE COMMAND)

Open Command Prompt and run:

```cmd
cd d:\Bhushan\parental
INSTALL.bat
```

**Wait 2-3 minutes** for it to build and install.

## STEP 3: Activate on Phone

1. On phone, open **"Device Setup"** app
2. Tap **"Activate Protection"**
3. Grant permissions when asked
4. App icon will disappear
5. Done! ✅

## STEP 4: View Dashboard

On any device, open browser:
```
https://remote-monitor-ktm5.onrender.com
```
